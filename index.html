What is this game about?

The Wormaid: The Wormaid is an indie puzzle game where you play as an exceedingly long worm-like creature seeking to unravel the mystery of your lost children. The puzzles follow the theme of space-filling (covering all tiles on a grid); however, the order and pattern of filling can reveal special symbols in the world that become a stepping path for the strange inhabitants of the game.
